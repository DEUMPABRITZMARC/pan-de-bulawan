About Us

Pan de Bulawan Bake Shop is a beloved local bakery that has been serving fresh, delicious, and affordable bread and cakes since 2021. Founded with passion and dedication, our shop is committed to using quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques to bring joy to every Filipino family. Owned and managed by Mr & Mrs Godfrey & Myrna Demetilla, Pande-Bulawan strives to be a warm, welcoming place where customers can enjoy baked goods made with heart, care, and a genuine love for the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bake and deliver fresh, delicious, and affordable bread and cakes using quality ingredients, while providing excellent customer service to our community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted and well-loved bakery in Zamboanga Sibugay, known for quality products, friendly service, and continuous growth to serve more customers in the future.
